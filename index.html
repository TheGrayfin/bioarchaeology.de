Dr. rer. nat. Angela Graefen

Bioarchaeology · Human Osteology · Paleopathology · Ancient DNA

About

Bioarchaeologist with a research focus on human osteology, paleopathology, and population genetics. Research interests include the reconstruction of health and disease in past populations, skeletal stress markers, and interdisciplinary approaches combining osteological, radiological, and molecular methods.
